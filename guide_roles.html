Tank

Tanks are high survivability heroes who can take a lot of damage. They occupy the forefront of battles, protecting their allies. Examples of heroes: Ax, Centaur Warrunner.

Support

Support are heroes whose task is to help their team. They buy map vision items, heal allies, and create control over enemies. Examples of heroes: Crystal Maiden, Dazzle.

Carry

Carries are heroes who start the game weak, but become stronger over time through farming and acquiring items. They are the main sources of damage in the late game. Examples of heroes: Juggernaut, Phantom Assassin.
