What is Dota 2?

Dota 2 is a popular massively multiplayer online game (MOBA) developed by Valve. The game involves two teams of five players fighting for control of the enemy base.

The main goals of the game are to destroy the enemy's "Throne" while protecting your own by controlling powerful heroes with unique abilities. Every Dota 2 game is a battle of strategies, tactics and teamwork.
